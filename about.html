About Us

Two Decades of Knitwear Excellence

Manufacturer & exporter of knitted garments for men, women and children — built on expertise, modern facilities and a dedicated technical team.

Our Story

From Yarn to Wardrobe, Under One Roof

Next Fashion Apparels carries a rich experience of over 20 years in knitted garment manufacturing. Our operations span the complete production chain — yarn selection, knitting, dyeing and finishing — with our stated objective being simple: to produce satisfactory products in demand for our clients.

We combine decades of manufacturing expertise with modern facilities and a dedicated technical team. Pre-allocated production planning ensures every order is executed punctually, and our streamlined supply chain enables shortened lead times with delivery in approximately 30 days, depending on style and order volume.
