Who We Are

Crafting Quality Knitwear From Yarn to Finish

Next Fashion Apparels manufactures and exports knitted garments for men, women and children, combining decades of manufacturing expertise with modern facilities and a dedicated technical team. Every stage — yarn selection, knitting, dyeing and finishing — is handled under strict quality oversight.

  • ✓ International standards — health & safety, labour laws, social responsibility
  • ✓ Pre-allocated production planning for punctual order execution
  • ✓ Efficient supply chain — shortened lead times, delivery in ~30 days
  • ✓ Connected by air, rail & road to Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad & Goa

What We Do

End-to-End Garment Manufacturing

A manufacturing unit equipped with hi-tech machines, serving diverse client needs under one roof.

🧶

Knitting & Fabric Processing

Premium yarn sourced from Kolhapur-region mills; knitting, dyeing and finishing overseen by technical personnel at our Islampur facility.

🖨️

Automatic Printing Unit

Grafica automatic printing — plastisol, pigment, non-PVC, high-density, discharge, photo, transfer, crack, foil, puff and rubber printing.

🪡

Embroidery & Embellishment

Machine and hand embroidery, laser-cut appliqué and custom embellishments to give every garment a distinctive finish.

✏️

In-House Design Team

Experienced designers using modern tools to create custom designs aligned with contemporary fashion trends.

✂️

Cutting & Stitching

Hi-tech cutting, printing and stitching machinery delivering excellent stitching, colour fastness and shrinkage-free construction.

🔍

Quality Assurance

A dedicated QA team with modern testing equipment verifies every garment is defect-free before delivery.
