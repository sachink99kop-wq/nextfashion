Knitting & Fabric Processing

We source premium yarn from the best mills in the Kolhapur region. Knitting operations are overseen by experienced technical personnel, while dyeing and fabric finishing are carried out at our Islampur facility.

Quality oversight runs across the entire chain — from the moment yarn is purchased until the garment is complete.

🖨️

Grafica Automatic Printing

Our automatic printing unit supports a wide range of techniques for every kind of design brief:

Plastisol Pigment Non-PVC High Density Discharge Photo Print Transfer Crack Foil Puff Rubber
🪡

Embroidery & Embellishment

Machine embroidery, delicate hand embroidery and laser-cut appliqué work — plus attachment of additional embellishments to give garments a premium, distinctive finish.

✏️

Design, Cutting & Stitching

Our in-house design team uses modern tools to develop custom designs aligned with contemporary fashion. The unit is equipped with hi-tech cutting, printing and stitching machinery to serve diverse client needs.

🔍

Quality Assurance

A dedicated quality assurance team, backed by modern testing equipment, checks colour fastness, tear strength and shrinkage — verifying every garment is defect-free before it leaves the facility.
